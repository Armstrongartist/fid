Background

Schooling
Peter got his first degree in Early Childhood Education before deciding to follow his passion for the arts. He went on to get his bachelor degree in Studio Art from the University of Central Florida. While on a medical break from work, he added a certificate in Graphic Design Production from Seminole State College of Floirda to his resume as well.

Personal History
Peter showed his love of the arts from an early age. Art & choir were always his favorite classes, and he often made art pieces for fun and as gifts. Peter discovered Bob Ross while watching PBS one day and found a new love for painting. Since then, he has only increased his skills and range of arts.

Current Life

Where
Peter currently still lives in Orlando, FL near his parents. Fun fact: he grew up two streets away from where Bob Ross himself used to live 😁

What
Peter is working as an art teacher for two separate programs and is in the process of getting his Art Teaching Certificate. This will allow him to teach art in schools in Florida. He also sells his art at galleries and on Etsy.com
